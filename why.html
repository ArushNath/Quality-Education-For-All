Why does this issue need to be resolved?

Have you ever gone to an Indian village? If you have, then you must've noticed the children living there, and realised that those children's lives will probably not be as bright as your's. But why? The reason behind this is because the chances are that this child's future is full of failure and unsuccess. They won't be able to survive in the world due to lack of knowledge and societal awareness, the very things they could develop if they received quality education.

Apart from that if more children receive quality education, then the amount of productive, working individuals will increase and global development will increase by a lot as a result. Did you know that according to the Times Of India, India makes up a third of the entire world's illiterate people! This is why it is so important to provide more people with quality education, especially in India.
