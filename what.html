What is the issue?

According to www.statista.com in 2020 the youth unemployment rate is 23.75%. That is more than 1/5th of the entire Indian Youth! So, why is employment important? More employment means more people who are willing to work and if more people work then the development rates will definitely go up. So what is the way to fix unemployment? The most practical way to increase employment is educating more people. But the issue is, that more than 70% of the Indian Population live in villages (according to The Seattle Times.) And most of the population that live in villages can't afford to send their young ones to good schools and hence they send their children to government schools.

The quality of education in Indian government schools is usually, terrible. Most teachers don't attend half of the time and are extremely underqualified to teach. Due to this children who go to government schools are extremely undereducated making it very hard for them to get a good job. The issue of children in India lacking quality education is increasing day by day yet is being paid very little attention. People talk about Pollution, Animal Cruelty, Racial Discrimination, etc. But barely anyone speaks about the issue to which this website is dedicated.
